## Current State

ClyptQ is live with **crypto trading** across 5 exchanges (Binance, Bybit, OKX, Gate.io, Coinbase), supporting spot and futures markets with 1-minute to 1-day OHLCV data. The platform includes 213+ operators, tick-by-tick backtesting, and backtest-to-live code parity.
